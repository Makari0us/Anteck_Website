Reverberation Room

A reverberation room is a specialized acoustic testing facility used for measuring sound power levels of sound sources and absorption coefficients of sound-absorbing materials. Its features include:

  • Highly reflective walls
  • Irregular shape design
  • Precise sound field diffusivity
  • International standard compliance
